ℹ️ Interference Theory

Quantum interference occurs when probability amplitudes from different paths combine. The total amplitude is the sum of individual amplitudes: ψ_total = ψ_1 + ψ_2. The probability is |ψ_total|² = |ψ_1|² + |ψ_2|² + 2Re(ψ_1*ψ_2).
